About Us

Our story, mission, and passion for baking

Our Story

AuIrphila Bakery was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create delicious, high-quality baked goods using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

What started as a small family-owned shop has grown into a beloved local institution, serving our community with passion and dedication for over a decade.

Our founder, Devanshi Sen, brought her grandmother's recipes from India and combined them with modern techniques to create our signature treats that customers have come to love.

Our Values

Quality Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients, organic when possible, to ensure exceptional flavor and quality in every bite.

Handcrafted Care

Each item is made by hand with attention to detail and the care that only comes from true craftsmanship.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from eco-friendly packaging to reducing food waste.

Community Focus

We believe in giving back to our community through partnerships with local charities and schools.
